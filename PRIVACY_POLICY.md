# Privacy Policy — TGSpeechBox

**Last updated:** February 24, 2026

TGSpeechBox is an open-source text-to-speech engine. It is designed to run entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

TGSpeechBox does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or telemetry
- No network connections
- No user accounts or sign-in
- No crash reporting
- No advertising or tracking

All speech synthesis is performed locally on your device. Text you enter is processed on-device and is never sent to any server.

## Permissions

TGSpeechBox requires no special permissions beyond standard text-to-speech service access provided by the operating system.

## Third-Party Services

TGSpeechBox does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
